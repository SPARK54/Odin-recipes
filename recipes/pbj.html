Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Two slices of bread filled with peanut butter and jelly

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread of your choice
  • Peanut butter, crucnhy or smooth
  • Jelly of your choice

Steps

  1. Using a butter knife, spread the peanut butter onto one slice of bread
  2. Using another butter knife, spread the jelly onto the other slice of bread
  3. Place one slice of bread on top of the other, with the peanut butter side facing the jelly side
  4. Enjoy!
