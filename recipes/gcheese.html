Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Delicious chedder cheese inside two toasted slices of bread, resulting in a crunchy outside and gooey, cheesy inside.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Slice cheese into small slices
  2. Place slices onto bread, covering everything but the crust
  3. Place another slice of bread onto the slice with cheese and place in a pan with medium heat
  4. Continue to flip the sandwich until cheese has fully melted and bread has seared lightly
